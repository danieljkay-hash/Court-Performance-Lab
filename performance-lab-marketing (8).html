The Platform
Everything your player needs.
All in one place.

Court Performance Lab gives coaches and parents full visibility into development — with AI-powered tools built for serious junior tennis.

🎥
Video Analysis

Upload match and practice footage. Get frame-by-frame technique breakdowns with AI-assisted coaching notes — serve mechanics, footwork, stroke patterns.

📊
Progress Dashboard

Track UTR progression, session history, development goals, and tournament results. Every player has a live profile coaches and parents can view anytime.

💬
Parent Consultations

Structured quarterly reviews, tournament planning sessions, and college pathway roadmaps — all documented, tracked, and accessible from the platform.

🎯
Court Annotation

Draw shot paths, mark positioning errors, and highlight tactical patterns directly on an interactive court diagram — shared instantly with players and parents.

📅
Session Scheduling

Book technique sessions, match play, and consultations in one place. Players get confirmation, session notes, and home practice assignments automatically.

✦
AI Coaching Assistant

Get real-time coaching recommendations, drill suggestions, match debriefs, and parent communication drafts — powered by AI trained on elite tennis development.
